ABOUT

How It Works

The LockBox was designed not only strong and attractive, but also straightforward, making it easy to install and use. Plus, it’s tamper-proof, weather-resistant, and made in America. Once packages are placed inside, the LockBox’s heavy-duty latch snaps shut and locks on contact, creating a secure point of delivery at homes and small businesses. Packages are kept safe until they can be retrieved at a convenient time. The LockBox is also app compatible, allowing owner's to remotely unlock and lock from anywhere, at anytime.

Friends and Family

Keep in mind LockBox isn’t only for commercial deliveries. Use LockBox to share and exchange things with friends and neighbors. Just place the item in your LockBox, use the app to remotely unlock the box for them, and let them pick it up at their convenience. When it’s time to return the item, they can just leave it in LockBox, where it will remain safe until you retrieve it.

LockBox Features

Commercial-grade locking mechanism designed to function smoothly.

Heavy-duty latch that snaps shut on contact.

Hydraulic struts that keep the lid propped open and prevent it from falling, making it safe and effortless to leave and retrieve packages.

Neatly ﬁnished, predrilled holes in the base are aligned with corresponding pre-drilled holes in the shoes, making for a faster installation.

Optional Installation

For the most secure installation of your LockBox, we suggest bolting it down to its location. You can attach LockBox to concrete, asphalt or wood, depending on where you want to place it. This may sound more difficult than it really is. Keep in mind you don’t have to bolt all 4 corners – one should do it.

Materials

LockBox’s main body is constructed from 16 gauge steel, a thickness that’s very difficult to penetrate. It’s then powder coated to created a smooth, slightly glossy ﬁnish that resists rusts and withstands the elements.

Capacity

At a roomy 9200 cubic inches (150 liters), LockBox is large enough to hold a dozen shoeboxes, but small enough to take home in your vehicle. LockBox’s generous, horizontally-oriented opening allows for packages to be placed inside, ensuring the contents arrive and remain safe.
